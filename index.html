At TEDxMACE 2023, we proudly present our theme for this year: "Retrouvaille." This French word, meaning "reconnec- tion," encapsulates the very essence of our event. "Retrouvaille" signifies the art of rediscovery, rekindling, and revi- talizing the connections that define our lives. Join us on an enriching journey where we honor the profound power of diverse connections, from the intricacies of human relationships to the vibrant threads of cultural ties, and our deep -rooted connection with the environment.

ABOUT TEDX MACE

TEDxMACE, organized at Mar Athanasius College of Engineering, Kothamangalam, is a dynamic and inspiring platform that has been igniting ideas, sparking conver- sations, and spreading knowledge for over six remarkable editions. Since its inception in 2016, TEDxMACE has been dedicated to fostering a culture of cu- riosity, critical thinking, and collabora- tion. Each edition has pushed boundaries and broken stereotypes, empowering the next generation of thought leaders and change-makers.

ABOUT TEDX

TEDx talks are conversations that aim at developing a local narrative while maintaining the underlying vision of TED. While TED takes more of a global approach, TEDx is a space that celebrates local voices. It's been the force that has taken TED across the planet and seeded all of these communities. Licensed from TED, it offers a wholesome experience to its speakers and attendees, a global stage for local dialogues. These conversations are recorded and later made available for free in TEDx handles.
